Data Structure & Algorithm

Data Structures and Algorithms (DS Algo) are essential for Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) students for several reasons. First, DS Algo provides a foundational understanding of how computer programs manipulate data and solve problems efficiently. Second, knowledge of DS Algo is essential for many technical interviews and coding challenges that CSE students may encounter during their studies or job search. Third, DS Algo concepts are widely used in various fields, including artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science.
